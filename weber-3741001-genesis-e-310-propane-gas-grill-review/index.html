Weber 3741001 Genesis E-310 Propane Gas Grill Review

Parts Available at eBay

Parts Available at Amazon

If you need a propane grill that delivers the best ability to grill your food, and you want that grill to last for many years to come, I think we've found the perfect grill for you! Yes, the Weber Genesis E-310 propane gas grill (model #3741001) is a little more expensive then most, but you really do get a lot of features and grilling abilities in the E-310 that you won't get in a cheaper grills that expect to be sold along with the Weber E-310 propane grill.

Starting from the bottom and moving up, the Weber 3741001 Genesis E-310 propane gas grill stands on a cart which is built with solid stainless steel doors and handles. Stainless steel keeps your grill from getting scratched as quickly, and cleans easily (an important feature for the bottom of a grill)! The propane tank resides within the cabinet, conveniently tucked away from site, keeping the stainless steel look intact.

The actual cooking area of the Weber Genesis E-310 gas grill is built with 3 high performance burners (42,000 BTU's), also of stainless steel. Weber wanted this grill to start quickly and easily, so they built an electronic system which lights all of the burners one after the other with the only input required from you is the push of a single button. Flavorizer bars are also built into the E-310, to keep the flames constant instead of being interrupted with grease splatter. The entire cooking area is a whopping 637 inches! That's split at a little more than 500" for the primary area with 130" or so left over for the warming rack.

To the right of the grilling surface are the controls for your Weber E-310, along with a decent sized area for extra tools and equipment. To the left of the grilling surface is another handy work area with built-in locations for your tools to drop into during the grilling process or after.

The Weber E310 propane gas grill is finished off up top with a conveniently mounted thermometer so you can easily tell the current temperature in your new grill. An all stainless steel grill that includes all of these features at this price? I think that's a bargain!
